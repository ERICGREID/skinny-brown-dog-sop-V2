Introduction & Overview
3

Core Principles

Skinny Brown Dog Media operates on a decentralized publishing model where authors maintain direct control over their earnings while we provide the infrastructure, expertise, and support to make their books successful.

Our Publishing Philosophy

  • Author-centric approach with transparent communication
  • Decentralized payment system for direct author control
  • Consistent quality control across all platforms
  • Scalable processes for efficient management
  • Documentation of all actions and decisions

Publishing System Overview

Our system utilizes three primary platforms:

1

BookVault

Direct sales platform where authors receive immediate payments through Stripe Connect or PayPal.

2

IngramSpark

Print-on-demand and global distribution to bookstores and libraries (excludes Amazon).

3

Amazon KDP

Distribution to Amazon marketplace, with optional KDP Select enrollment for promotional opportunities.

Your Role as Publishing Manager

As a Skinny Brown Dog Media employee, you are responsible for:

  • Guiding authors through the publishing process
  • Setting up and managing distribution accounts
  • Ensuring file quality and platform compliance
  • Facilitating author payments and reporting
  • Providing marketing support and guidance
  • Troubleshooting issues as they arise

Remember: Excellent communication and attention to detail are essential to our success and our authors' satisfaction.

BookVault Setup & Management
5

Initial Account Setup

1

Team Member Invitation

  1. Log in to BookVault Publisher Dashboard
  2. Navigate to Team > Invite New Member
  3. Enter author's email with the following permissions:
    • ✓ View Sales
    • ✓ View Books
    • ✓ View Reports
    • ✓ Receive Payments
    • ✗ Edit Files (unchecked)
  4. Click "Send Invite" button
  5. Confirm author receives the invitation
2

Payment Setup Guidance

Guide author through payment setup process:

  • Stripe Connect: Business verification (requires SSN/EIN)
  • PayPal: Connection to existing account
  • Confirm setup completion before publication

File Upload Guidelines

Interior PDF Requirements

  • PDF/X-1a:2001 compliant
  • No crop marks
  • Correct trim size
  • All fonts embedded
  • 300 DPI minimum for images
  • Proper margins (min 0.5" all sides)

Cover PDF Requirements

  • 0.125" bleed on all sides
  • CMYK color mode
  • 300 DPI minimum
  • Correct spine width
  • ISBN barcode on back cover
  • All text in safe zones

Metadata & Pricing

Complete all metadata fields accurately:

  • Title & Subtitle: Exactly as they appear on the cover
  • Author Name: Exactly as it appears on the cover
  • Description: Compelling 150-200 word summary
  • Categories: Select primary and secondary
  • Keywords: 5-7 relevant search terms
  • Pricing: Competitive for genre, ensuring adequate margins

BookVault Quality Control

  • Always order a proof copy for author review
  • Test the purchase process to confirm payment flows to author
  • Verify book appears correctly on the Skinny Brown Dog Media storefront
  • Take screenshots of the final listing for documentation

Important: Always verify the author has successfully completed Stripe Connect or PayPal setup before publishing.

IngramSpark Setup & Management
6

Account Creation

1

Initial Setup

  1. Create new account using:
    • Author's email address
    • Skinny Brown Dog Media as publisher name
    • Assign ISBN from our bulk inventory
  2. Document account details in the IngramSpark & BookVault Upload Log
  3. Share login credentials with author securely

Title Setup Process

2

Book Details

Configure title settings:

  • Print Options:
    • Paper color (white/cream)
    • Paper weight (typically 50# for fiction, 60# for nonfiction)
    • Cover finish (gloss/matte)
  • Metadata:
    • Complete all BISAC categories
    • Enter thorough book description
    • Add comprehensive author bio
3

Critical Distribution Settings

Configure these settings exactly as shown:

  • Global Distribution: ✓ ENABLED
  • Amazon Expanded Distribution: ✗ DISABLED (MUST be unchecked)
  • Wholesale Discount: 40% (standard)
  • Returns: No returns (unless author requests otherwise)

IngramSpark File Requirements

Files must meet these specifications:

  • Interior: PDF/X-1a:2001 or PDF/X-3:2002 compliant
  • Cover: CMYK color mode, 300+ DPI, includes bleed area
  • Complete the File Upload QA Checklist before submission

CRITICAL: Distribution Settings

Never check "Amazon Expanded Distribution" - this creates conflicts with direct KDP listings and can result in book removal from Amazon.

Proofing & Approval

  • Always order physical proof copies (1 for author, 1 for office records)
  • Send proof copies to author using trackable shipping
  • Require completed Proof Approval Form before approving for distribution
  • Document approval in the IngramSpark & BookVault Upload Log

Pro Tip: If IngramSpark rejects files, use the specialized screencast tutorial "How to Troubleshoot IngramSpark Rejections" to quickly resolve common issues.

File Preparation & Quality Control
8

Interior File Standards

Formatting Requirements

  • Consistent margins (min 0.5" all sides)
  • Page numbers in appropriate position
  • Chapter breaks on right-hand pages
  • Proper front matter (title, copyright, etc.)
  • Proper back matter (about author, etc.)
  • Consistent paragraph formatting

Technical Requirements

  • PDF/X-1a:2001 compliant
  • All fonts embedded
  • Images at minimum 300 DPI
  • CMYK color mode for color interiors
  • No crop marks or printer marks
  • Correct page size (matches trim size)

Cover File Standards

Covers must meet these specifications:

  • Design Requirements:
    • Professional appearance
    • Genre-appropriate
    • Readable title/author name at thumbnail size
    • Appropriate contrast and color scheme
  • Technical Requirements:
    • 0.125" bleed area on all sides
    • CMYK color mode (not RGB)
    • 300 DPI minimum resolution
    • Spine width calculated based on page count
    • ISBN barcode on back cover
    • All important elements within safe zone (0.25" from trim)

EPUB File Creation

eBook files must be properly formatted:

  • Valid EPUB 3.0 format that passes validation
  • Proper navigation structure and table of contents
  • All links functional (internal and external)
  • Alt text for all images
  • Metadata correctly embedded
  • Cover image displays in library view

Quality Control Process

1

File Review

Complete the File Upload QA Checklist for each file type:

  • Interior PDF (margins, fonts, images, etc.)
  • Cover PDF (bleed, spine, barcode, etc.)
  • EPUB file (navigation, links, validation)
2

Platform Preview

Use platform-specific preview tools:

  • IngramSpark's PDF proof viewer
  • KDP's online previewer
  • Kindle Previewer for eBooks
3

Physical Proof Review

  • Order physical proof copies from all platforms
  • Conduct internal review for quality issues
  • Send author copies for review and approval
  • Collect completed Proof Approval Form

Payment Systems & Management
10

Payment System Overview

Our decentralized payment model ensures authors receive direct payments from each platform:

1

BookVault/Stripe Connect

  • Author receives payment directly to their bank account
  • Payment frequency: Typically 2-7 business days after sale
  • Transaction fee: Stripe's standard fee (approximately 2.9% + $0.30)
  • Minimum payout threshold: None
2

IngramSpark

  • Author receives payment directly from IngramSpark
  • Payment frequency: 90 days after the end of the sales month
  • Minimum payout threshold: $25
  • Payment methods: Direct deposit or check
3

Amazon KDP

  • Author receives payment directly from Amazon
  • Payment frequency: 60 days after the end of the sales month
  • Minimum payout threshold: Varies by country ($10-$100)
  • Payment methods: Direct deposit, check, or wire transfer

Author Payment Setup

Required steps for each platform:

  • Stripe Connect:
    • Guide authors through verification process
    • Help with business entity setup (Sole Proprietor/LLC)
    • Verify connection with test purchase
  • PayPal (alternative):
    • Connect to author's existing PayPal account
    • Verify connection is working
  • IngramSpark/KDP:
    • Assist with bank information entry
    • Help with tax documentation completion

Tax Documentation

Required forms and procedures:

  • U.S. Authors: W-9 form required
  • International Authors: W-8BEN form typically required
  • Store all tax forms securely in encrypted storage
  • Remind authors about 1099 reporting requirements

Explaining Payment Timelines

Set clear expectations with authors about payment timing:

  • BookVault/Stripe: Within days of sale
  • IngramSpark: 90+ days after month-end
  • Amazon KDP: 60+ days after month-end

Help authors understand that different sales channels have different payment schedules.

Important: Never provide tax advice to authors. Always recommend they consult with a qualified tax professional for specific guidance.

Troubleshooting Common Issues
12

File Rejection Issues

1

IngramSpark File Rejection

Common causes and solutions:

  • Bleed issues: Ensure cover extends 0.125" beyond trim edge
  • Margin issues: Minimum 0.5" margin required on all sides
  • Image resolution: All images must be 300+ DPI
  • Font embedding: All fonts must be embedded in the PDF
  • Spine width: Recalculate based on exact page count
2

KDP File Rejection

Common causes and solutions:

  • Content outside printable area: Check margins
  • Low-resolution images: Replace with 300+ DPI
  • Spine text issues: Ensure minimum page count for spine text
  • ISBN barcode problems: Generate new barcode at correct size
  • EPUB validation errors: Use EPUB validator to identify issues

Payment Issues

1

Stripe Connection Problems

Troubleshooting steps:

  • Verify author completed all verification steps
  • Check for expired cards or banking information
  • Verify business name matches tax documentation
  • Contact Stripe support if necessary
2

Missing Payments

Investigation process:

  • Verify payment was actually issued
  • Check payment thresholds and timing
  • Verify tax documentation is complete
  • Document in Royalty Payout Issues Tracker

Distribution Delays

1

Books Not Appearing on Retailers

Resolution steps:

  • Check distribution settings
  • Verify no conflicting listings exist
  • Allow standard timeframe (1-2 weeks for most retailers)
  • Contact platform support if exceeding normal timeframes
2

Amazon/IngramSpark Conflicts

Prevention and resolution:

  • ALWAYS uncheck "Amazon Expanded Distribution" in IngramSpark
  • ALWAYS uncheck "Expanded Distribution" in KDP
  • If conflict occurs, remove the book from one platform
  • Allow 5-7 days for channel conflict to resolve

Escalation Procedures

  1. Level 1: Try standard troubleshooting steps
  2. Level 2: Consult with senior team member
  3. Level 3: Escalate to management
  4. Level 4: Executive intervention for critical issues

Author Exit Process
13

Exit Scenarios

Common reasons for author exit:

  • Contract term completion (typically 3 years)
  • Author request to terminate agreement
  • Publisher decision to terminate agreement
  • Mutual agreement to end relationship

Exit Workflow

1

Initial Documentation

Required steps:

  • Complete Author Exit/Termination Checklist
  • Document reason for exit
  • Verify all contractual terms met by both parties
  • Set timeline for completion of exit process
2

Distribution Management

Platform actions:

  • BookVault: Remove book from store or transfer to author account
  • IngramSpark: Remove from distribution or transfer to author account
  • KDP: Transfer account access or remove listings
  • Document completion of each step with date and confirmation numbers
3

ISBN Management

ISBN handling options:

  • ISBN remains with Skinny Brown Dog Media (standard)
  • ISBN transferred to author (additional fee may apply)
  • New ISBN provided to author for republication
  • Document transfer with proper paperwork
4

File Delivery

Transfer to author:

  • Interior print-ready PDF
  • Cover print-ready PDF
  • EPUB file
  • Original manuscript
  • Marketing materials
  • Verify receipt by author

Financial Closure

  • Generate final sales reports for all platforms
  • Verify all outstanding royalties paid
  • Unlink Stripe Connect account
  • Unlink PayPal account if applicable
  • Process any exit fees if applicable
  • Document completion of financial closure

Special Circumstances

Additional considerations for certain situations:

  • Co-authored books: Verify agreement between all parties
  • Series books: Clarify rights for entire series
  • Books with licensed content: Address third-party permissions
  • Disputed terminations: Consult legal counsel

Important: Always complete and retain the Author Exit/Termination Checklist in the author's file and provide a copy to the author for their records.
